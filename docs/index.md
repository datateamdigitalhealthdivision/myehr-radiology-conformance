# National Radiology Conformance Chapters

This directory contains the narrative chapters that sit alongside the FHIR implementation guide. Together they define how Malaysian radiology interoperability is expected to work across FHIR, DICOM, DICOMweb, and IHE-aligned workflow patterns.

## Editorial conventions

- `PROVISIONAL` means the design direction is drafted but not finally approved.
- `TO BE CONFIRMED` means a national decision is still required.
- `EXAMPLE ONLY` means the content is illustrative and not a mandated production value.
- `DRAFT POLICY OPTION` means more than one nationally acceptable route remains open.
